Frequently Asked Questions

Who can benefit from ABY Therapy? +

ABY Therapy is designed for anyone seeking mental health support, whether you're dealing with specific challenges like anxiety or depression, going through life transitions, or simply wanting to improve your overall well-being. Our services are suitable for adults, teens, couples, and families.

How does online therapy work? +

Online therapy works through secure video calls, phone sessions, or messaging platforms. You'll be matched with a licensed therapist based on your needs and preferences. Sessions are conducted in real-time just like traditional therapy, but from the comfort and privacy of your chosen location.

Do you take insurance? +

We work with most major insurance providers and also offer affordable self-pay options. Our team will help verify your insurance benefits and explain your coverage options during the initial consultation. We believe cost should never be a barrier to getting the help you need.

Is my privacy and safety protected? +

Absolutely. We use HIPAA-compliant, encrypted platforms to ensure complete confidentiality. All our therapists are bound by professional ethical codes, and we have strict privacy policies in place. Your personal information and session content are never shared without your explicit consent.
