Our Treatment Approaches

We utilize evidence-based therapeutic modalities tailored to each client's unique needs and goals.

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

Identify and change negative thought patterns and behaviors that contribute to emotional distress.

Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT)

Develop skills in mindfulness, emotion regulation, distress tolerance, and interpersonal effectiveness.

Psychodynamic Therapy

Explore unconscious patterns and childhood experiences that influence current thoughts and behaviors.

Humanistic Therapy

Focus on self-exploration, personal growth, and realizing your full potential.

EMDR

Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing for trauma and PTSD treatment.

Mindfulness-Based Therapies

Cultivate present-moment awareness to reduce stress and improve emotional regulation.
